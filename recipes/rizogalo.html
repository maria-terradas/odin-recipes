Rizogalo

Description

Recipe by Live Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 140g rice arborio
  • 490g water
  • 120g white sugar
  • 1 liter whole milk
  • 80g corn flour
  • Zest from 2 oranges
  • 1 tbsp. vanilla extract
  • 50g cow butter

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, boil the rice (in water) for 15-20 minutes with the lid closed.
  2. As soon as the rice is pureed, add the sugar and mix.
  3. Add the milk and vanilla and mix.
  4. Turn up the heat to full and add the corn flour.
  5. Stir continuously with a whisk until the mixture thickens.
  6. As soon as the mixture thickens, remove from the heat, add the zest and butter and mix until the butter melts.
  7. Serve in cups and eat it hot or put it in the fridge to cool.
